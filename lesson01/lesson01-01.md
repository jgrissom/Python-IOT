## Battery Driven LED Circuit

#### Materials
 - 2.25" x 1 red wire
 - 1.00" x 3 red wire
 - 1.00" x 1 black wire
 - LED x 1 red
 - 330 Ohm resistor x 1

[Circuit Drawing](lesson01-01.pdf)

#### Instructions
 - Cut & strip wires
 - Assemble circuit using provided breadboard
 - Test circuit by toggling switch
